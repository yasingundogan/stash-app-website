üîê How secure is my luggage? +

Your belongings are stored in secure facilities with 24/7 surveillance, individual tracking codes, and full insurance coverage. Each item is verified during drop-off and pick-up to ensure maximum security.

üí∞ How much does it cost? +

Pricing starts from just $5 per hour or $20 per day for standard items. Larger items and extended storage periods have special rates. You only pay for the time you actually use - no hidden fees!

üì¶ What items can I store? +

You can store luggage, backpacks, shopping bags, sports equipment, laptops, and important documents. We cannot accept hazardous materials, perishable food, illegal items, or anything that violates safety regulations.

‚è∞ What are the operating hours? +

Operating hours vary by location and partner. Most locations operate from 8 AM to 10 PM daily. You can check specific hours for each location in our app before booking.

üì± Do I need to download an app? +

While our mobile app provides the best experience, you can also book through our website. The app offers features like real-time notifications, easy pick-up code access, and faster booking.

üîÑ Can I cancel or modify my booking? +

Yes! You can cancel or modify your booking up to 1 hour before your scheduled drop-off time for a full refund. Changes after that may incur a small fee depending on the circumstances.

üõ°Ô∏è What if my item gets damaged or lost? +

All items are covered by our comprehensive insurance policy up to $1,000 per item. For high-value items, you can purchase additional coverage during booking. We take full responsibility for items in our care.

üìç How do I find a storage location? +

Simply open our app or website, enter your current location or destination, and we'll show you all nearby Stash locations with real-time availability. You can filter by distance, price, and operating hours.
